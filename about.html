In my work, the relationships between people and their environments is explored. How do our surroundings shape our experiences and state of mind? Taking from my own experiences growing up in Northwest Indiana, the Midwest is a commonly investigated element as I navigate themes of isolation, transition, and the exploration of self.

Using classical techniques in oils and with a strong commitment to realism, I strive to create accurate representations of my subjects. Juxtaposing the figure with a landscape creates an element of narrative mystery, and invites the viewer to contemplate the relationship with their own environment.
