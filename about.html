My work primarily focuses on the relationships between people and their environments, exploring how our surroundings shape our experiences and state of mind. Narrative is an important element in my paintings, as well as themes of isolation, transitions and exploring oneâ€™s self. Taking from my experiences growing up in northwest Indiana, investigating the surroundings of the Midwest is a common element in many of my works.

A strong commitment to realism is at the core of my paintings. Taking inspiration from the work of the Italian Renaissance masters, as well as many contemporary artists working today, I strive to create as accurate of a representation of my subject as possible, using classical oil painting techniques. Juxtaposing the figure with a landscape of my choosing creates an element of narrative and mystery in the paintings.
